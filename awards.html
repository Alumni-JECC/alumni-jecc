Jyothi Engineering College Alumni Association (JECAA)

Best Jyothian Award

Best Jyothian Award is sponsored by Jyothi Engineering College Alumni Association and was started in 2016. The aim is to recognize and award outstanding students based on leadership and ethical values, co-curricular activities, academic performance and professional skill. A certificate and an amount of Rs. 5,000/- will be awarded to the selected student. Mr. Varun Nellore of Mechanical Engineering (2013-17 passout batch) was the 1st winner of this award and was given during the College day.

Alumni Scholarships

  1. 2002- 2006 EEE batch giving an award for Best outgoing EEE student.
  2. Every year 2007-2011 ME batch is providing an endowment to a needy student from the academic year 2015-16 onwards.
  3. 2010-2014 AEI batch is sponsoring a student from 2017-2021 batch.
